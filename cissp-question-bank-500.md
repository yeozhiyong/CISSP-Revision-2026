## DOMAIN 1: SECURITY & RISK MANAGEMENT (Q1-80)

**Q1.** A company has an asset valued at $500,000. Analysis shows a potential loss of 30% once every 5 years. What is the Annual Loss Expectancy (ALE)?

A) $30,000 ✓  
B) $150,000  
C) $75,000  
D) $50,000  

*SLE = $500,000 × 0.30 = $150,000. ARO = 1/5 = 0.2. ALE = $150,000 × 0.2 = $30,000.*

**Q2.** Which risk response strategy involves purchasing insurance?

A) Risk Avoidance  
B) Risk Mitigation  
C) Risk Transfer ✓  
D) Risk Acceptance  

*Risk transfer shifts the financial impact to a third party (insurance).*
