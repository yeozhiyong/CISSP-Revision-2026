## 🎴 Flashcards - CIA Triad

| Front | Back |
|-------|------|
| **Confidentiality** | Prevent unauthorized **disclosure** (encryption, access controls) |
| **Integrity** | Prevent unauthorized **modification** (hashing, digital signatures) |
| **Availability** | Ensure **timely, reliable access** (redundancy, backups, failover) |

---

## 🎴 Flashcards - Policy Hierarchy

| Front | Back |
|-------|------|
| **Policy** | **High-level**, strategic, approved by senior management ("We protect data") |
| **Standard** | **Specific, mandatory** requirements ("Passwords must be 12+ chars") |
| **Procedure** | **Step-by-step** instructions ("How to reset password") |
| **Guideline** | **Optional** recommendations, best practices |

---

## 🎴 Flashcards - Risk Formulas

| Front | Back |
|-------|------|
| **Risk = ?** | Threat × Vulnerability × Impact |
| **SLE = ?** | Asset Value × EF (Single Loss Expectancy) |
| **ALE = ?** | SLE × ARO (Annual Loss Expectancy) |
| **EF** | Exposure Factor (% of loss if event occurs) |
| **ARO** | Annualized Rate of Occurrence (times per year) |

---

## 🎴 Flashcards - Risk Response Strategies

| Front | Back |
|-------|------|
| **Avoid** | Eliminate the risk (stop the activity) |
| **Mitigate** | Reduce likelihood/impact (controls, training) |
| **Transfer** | Shift financial impact (**insurance**, outsourcing) |
| **Accept** | Do nothing, absorb cost (when mitigation cost > risk) |

---

## 🎴 Flashcards - Key Concepts

| Front | Back |
|-------|------|
| **Due Care** | Taking **reasonable steps** to protect (action) |
| **Due Diligence** | **Continuous monitoring** and review (ongoing) |
| **Residual Risk** | Risk **remaining after** controls implemented |
| **Total Risk** | Risk **before any** controls (inherent + residual) |

## 📝 Quick Calculation Example

**Scenario:** Asset $100K, EF 40%, every 5 years
- **SLE** = $100K × 0.40 = **$40,000**
- **ARO** = 1/5 = **0.2**
- **ALE** = $40K × 0.2 = **$8,000 per year**

**Decision:** Spend $15K on protection?
**Answer:** **Yes** (if < SLE or < ALE over time)
