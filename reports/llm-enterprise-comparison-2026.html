Key Trends and Insights

1. Cost Collapse

LLM costs have dropped 10-20x since 2024. Kimi K2.5 and GPT-5.4 nano make AI accessible to smaller organizations.

2. Context Window Explosion

All major models now offer 100K+ context, with Gemini leading at 1M tokens. This enables processing entire codebases or books.

3. Specialization Over Generalization

The market is segmenting: GPT-5.4 for general tasks, Claude for safety, Kimi for cost, Gemini for multimodal.

4. Multi-Model Strategy Emerges

Smart organizations use multiple models rather than defaulting to the most expensive option.

Conclusion

The LLM market in 2026 offers unprecedented choice. While GPT-5.4 maintains the performance crown, cost-performance leaders like Kimi K2.5 offer compelling alternatives for most applications.
